## Brand Direction

The brand should feel professional, durable, contractor-trusted, and modern enough to support automated online estimates and a customer job app. Avoid gimmicky desert tropes, cheap contractor visuals, generic stock-photo polish, and overly luxury styling that feels disconnected from real epoxy/concrete work.

Core tone: skilled, direct, reliable, pro-grade, locally responsive, and process-driven.

## Three Template Style Directions

1. Style A: Pro Contractor Funnel
   - Default Phoenix owner-review direction.
   - Bold dark/neutral base, bright blue/copper accents, strong CTA, process section, trust strip.

2. Style B: Premium Garage/Homeowner
   - Residential garage, patio, and home buyer direction.
   - Cleaner, brighter, more lifestyle-forward, with before/after gallery emphasis.

3. Style C: Training + Business Starter
   - Epoxy contractors, adjacent industries, and people starting epoxy businesses.
   - XPS training authority, tools/products, class inquiry, and business-opportunity messaging.
